# AeroEngine-Hydrogen-Injector
Thermal and Combustion analysis of hydrogen fuel injectors for aircraft gas turbine engines. Includes simulation results, performance data, and comparative study with conventional injector designs.
The Scope of this project was to develop 2 different aero-engine Hydrogen fuel injectors having good mixing and low NOx parts per million.
SOLDIWORKS and CATIA were used to develop the design of the injector models used and ANSYS Fluent was used to simulate them
It was found that an injector model with swirl geometry produced 3 times lower NOx than an injector without swirl geometry but lost 30% of its chamber pressure compared to 7% on the second model.
The injector design employing the swirl model is more promisable, since further design optimization can improve the pressure loss.
